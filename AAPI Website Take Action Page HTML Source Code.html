What else can you do?

Speak with your elected officials! Is there a bill supporting the AAPI community that your elected official can help pass? Is there a certain issue in your area that they aren't addressing? Reach out to them.

Call for Ethnic Studies courses in your school! These programs will help educate many more students on the history of racism against the AAPI community.

Become a customer at a local AAPI business!

Share places for members of the AAPI community to report any hate incidents.
